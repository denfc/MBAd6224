The Ancient Concept Known as a Book

No book is assigned for this course. In this open-source, internet era, the transmission of information occurs in many different ways and as much as I like books, I see no need to make you buy one when the material for which you may need explanation(s) is available for free, or in the case of the second book reference below, for $2.99 (well worth it). As a way to help your fellow students, we should set up a reference page in Blackboard, across the entire course, where people can share resources they found helpful. (dfc 15 July 2016)

  2. Book 2: The Little Handbook of Statistical Practice
  3. Book 3: Online Statistics Education: An Interactive Multimedia Course of Study
